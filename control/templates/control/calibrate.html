SO-101 Physical Calibration Guide

Important: Proper calibration ensures leader and follower arms have identical position values when in the same physical position. This is critical for neural network training across different robots.

Leader arm uses different gearing to sustain its weight while allowing easy manual movement.

Calibration Steps
  1. Position to Zero: Move both robot arms to the position where all joints are in the middle of their ranges.
  2. Press Enter: After confirming the position, press Enter to record the zero position.
  3. Full Range Motion: Move each joint through its full range of motion when prompted.
  4. Verify Alignment: Ensure both leader and follower arms report identical position values when physically aligned.
